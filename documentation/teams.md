# Teams

## Overview

**Teams** are groups of **Users** who collaborate on projects. Being a member of a team allows for seamless sharing of resources, including **Projects**, **Prompts**, **Codebooks**, and **Runs**, with other team members. This is designed to facilitate collaboration on shared research goals and to ensure consistency across a group's work.

Teams are also the unit of **Billing** in Sandpiper. Each team has a credit balance that is consumed when LLM runs are executed. Team administrators can purchase credits via Stripe and monitor usage across all team members and projects.

Users can be a part of multiple teams, allowing for participation in various collaborative efforts.

## How to use

### Creating and Managing a Team

1.  **Create a Team:** Navigate to the "Teams" section and click "Create Team." Enter a name for your team.
2.  **Invite Members:** Generate an invite link to share with other **Users**. Members who click the link will be added to the team.
3.  **Manage Members:** From the team detail page, view and manage team members in the "Users" tab.

### Team Resources

All core resources in Sandpiper are scoped to a team:

| Resource        | Description                                              |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Projects**    | Create and manage projects within the team               |
| **Prompts**     | Shared prompt library accessible to all team members     |
| **Codebooks**   | Shared codebook definitions for consistent coding        |
| **Billing**     | View credit balance, purchase credits, and track costs   |

### Team Billing

1.  **View Balance:** Navigate to the "Billing" tab on your team page to see your current credit balance and usage.
2.  **Purchase Credits:** Click "Add Credits" to purchase additional credits via Stripe.
3.  **Monitor Costs:** Review cost breakdowns by model, by source (annotation, verification, adjudication), and over time.
